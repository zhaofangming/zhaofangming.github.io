Biography

Fangming Zhao received the B.E. degree in communication engineering from the School of Information Engineering, Guangdong University of Technology, Guangzhou, China, in 2019, and the M.S. degree (Hons.) in information and communication engineering from the School of Electronics and Communication Engineering, Sun Yat-sen University, Shenzhen Campus, Shenzhen, China, in 2022. He is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with Zhejiang University-University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign Institute (ZJU-UIUC Institute). He is also with the College of Information Science and Electronic Engineering, Zhejiang University, China. His research interests include age of information, green communications, and stochastic modeling of wireless networks.
